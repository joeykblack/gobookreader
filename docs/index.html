    About

    Version: checking…

    GoBooks Reader is built for studying Go books actively, not just reading passively. It lets you open EPUB-based Go books and turn important problem sections into scheduled reviews.

    Unlike a regular EPUB reader, this app adds spaced repetition (SRS) on top of your reading. In a normal reader, you may forget a problem pattern after a few days. Here, each marked section is brought back at the right time so you can reinforce reading patterns and tesuji memory.

    New to SRS? SRS means reviewing right before you are likely to forget. After each review, you rate difficulty with buttons like Again, Hard, Good, or Easy. The scheduler then picks the next due time:

    • Again: show soon (you need quick reinforcement).
    • Hard: show sooner than normal.
    • Good: normal progress.
    • Easy: wait longer before next review.

    Over time, this helps you remember more shapes and answers with less total review time than re-reading chapters randomly.
